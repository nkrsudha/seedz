About

THRIVE ACUPUNCTURE

Thrive Acupuncture is the manifestation of an exploration of the benefits of feeling good. Be it physical or emotional, our happiness is compromised when we experience pain and discomfort. From your first consultation, we will work together to identify the areas of your health that are of concern and the origin of these imbalances. Using acupuncture, herbal formulas, nutritional counseling, bodywork, yoga, and other therapeutic techniques, we will address these concerns, restoring the body to it’s optimal state of wellness and working to maintain this equilibrium long after your treatments are complete.

At Thrive Acupuncture, the emphasis is on the individual, with focus on creating lasting change from the inside out. I do not believe in one-size-fits-all health care, nor do I believe that what works one day will work the next. I do believe that real change requires adaptability and commitment, and addressing problems at the root will heed the best results. My goal as a practitioner is to give you the most comprehensive care possible, working within the realistic parameters of your lifestyle and my abilities.
