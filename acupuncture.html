Acupuncture

WHAT IS ACUPUNCTURE?

Acupuncture is a systematic method of bringing the body into balance through the insertion of small sterile needles into a series of points that run along the channels of the body. The points are selected based on the pattern that each individual presents with at the time of treatment. It is used to facilitate the body’s natural healing process, bringing the individual into a state of equilibrium so that the body can function at it’s most optimum, repairing and restoring the body to a place of health.

 

WHAT DOES ACUPUNCTURE TREAT?

Acupuncture is used to treat a wide range of conditions, including but not limited to:

-Musculoskeletal Pain
-Joint Pain
-Headaches
-Depression
-Anxiety
-Insomnia
-Allergies
-Reproductive Challenges
-Gynecological Problems
-Stress
-Fibromyalgia
-Poor Digestion
-Skin Conditions
-Respiratory Issues
-Post-Operative Healing
-General Support/Side Effects from Cancer Treatments
-Bell’s Palsy
